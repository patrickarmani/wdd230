Rule of Third

Tesla

tesla.com

According to the Digital Photography School website the definition of rule of third is " is a compositional guideline that breaks an image down into thirds (both horizontally and vertically) so you have nine pieces and four gridlines."
Tesla's page does this very well with the vehicle photos that appear dominating the entire site. When you look to their page, it is graphically balanced, maintaining harmony and simplicity without losing elegance.

White Space and Clean Design

Junesse Global

Jeunesse Global

According to the Webflow Blog "White Space and Clean Design, is a part of almost everything we experience, and it plays a crucial role in how we perceive art, design, and communication."
Jeunesse Global is a company that develops high-tech products to enhance youth, enhance beauty and promote health. With the use of White Space and Clean Design, it gives the website visitor the same sensation that the products can provide: lightness, prominence and focus.

Contrast

Binance

binance.com

According to the Boia website, "the concept of color contrast is as critical as it is simple. Color contrast refers to the difference in light between font (or anything in the foreground) and its background." or n other words, a website with good contrast between background and elements, can make the visitor experience more interesting and make the message understood clearly. On the Binanc website, for example, the contrast between orange and black makes it easy to read and breaks the usual pattern of black and white in texts.
